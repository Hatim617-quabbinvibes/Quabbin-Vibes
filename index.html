About Quabbin Vibes

Professional media coverage of Western & Central New England events

Our Mission

Quabbin Vibes is a professional media platform dedicated to comprehensive coverage of events and venues across Western and Central New England. We provide high-quality photography, detailed event reviews, and serve as the premier resource for regional event discovery.

Our coverage area spans from Worcester to Pittsfield and Brattleboro to Hartford, encompassing the cultural heart of Massachusetts, Vermont, New Hampshire, and Connecticut.
